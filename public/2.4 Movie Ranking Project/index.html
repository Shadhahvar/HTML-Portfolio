Top Movies of All Time According to Matthew

My best 3 movies

The Village

No movie better conveys Halloween's every ounce of mood and atmosphere better than this one, and it does it all without a single witch, jack-o-lantern, or any of the other usual tropes.

Tenet

A film that experiments, and even plays, with every facet of film as a story telling medium, and succeeds and surprises in each of its aims.

The Thing

What masquerades as the pinnacle of practical effects horror is revealed, on closer inspection, to in fact be the greatest who-dunnit of all time.
